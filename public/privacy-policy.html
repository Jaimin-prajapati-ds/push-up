Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Overview

PushChamp ("the App") is a fitness application that uses your device's camera and AI pose detection to count push-up and sit-up repetitions. Your privacy is our top priority.

2. Data Collection

Camera Data: The App accesses your front-facing camera ONLY for real-time pose detection. All camera processing happens entirely ON YOUR DEVICE. No camera images, video, or pose data is ever transmitted to any server.

Workout Data: Your workout history (reps, duration, calories) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Profile Data: Any profile information you enter (name, weight, height) is stored locally on your device only.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information (like device identifiers) to show relevant ads.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions (if applicable).
  • Google Fonts: Used for typography.

4. Internet Usage

The App requires internet access for the initial download of AI models, displaying advertisements, and checking subscription status.

5. Data Storage & Deletion

Most data is stored in your device's local storage. You can delete local data at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
