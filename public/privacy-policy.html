Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. Overview

PushChamp ("the App") is a fitness application that uses your device's camera and AI pose detection to count push-up repetitions. Your privacy is our top priority.

2. Data Collection

Camera Data: The App accesses your front-facing camera ONLY for real-time pose detection. All camera processing happens entirely ON YOUR DEVICE using TensorFlow.js. No camera images, video, or pose data is ever transmitted to any server.

Workout Data: Your workout history (reps, duration, calories) is stored locally on your device using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Profile Data: Any profile information you enter (name, weight, height) is stored locally on your device only.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No camera images or recordings
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising data
  • No data shared with third parties

4. Internet Usage

The App requires internet access only for the initial download of the AI pose detection model (TensorFlow.js MoveNet). After the first load, the model is cached and works offline.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Fonts for typography. No personal data is shared with Google through this usage.

6. Data Storage & Deletion

All data is stored in your device's local storage. You can delete all data at any time through Settings → Reset All Data, or by uninstalling the app.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. No Advertisements

PushChamp is a paid app and contains NO advertisements whatsoever.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
